Yang Chen (陈阳) currently pursues a Ph.D. in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems at Nanjing Normal University under the supervision of Guoan Tang and Xin Yang. Yang’s research interests include urban modeling, 3D urban morphology, and artificial landscapes (landforms). Proficient in Python, JavaScript, C#, SQL, and GIS software like ArcGIS and QGIS, Yang has teaching experience as an assistant in GIS principles and has received multiple academic awards. Fieldwork experience includes UAV surveying in Nanjing and field investigations in China’s Loess Plateau.
